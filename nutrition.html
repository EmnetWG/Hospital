Nutritional Assessment Package

Our expert-led nutritional assessment is a personalised approach to healthcare that helps prevent or treat nutrition-related illnesses and diseases, including diabetes, cardiovascular disease and cancer. A nutritional assessment can highlight any shortfalls in your diet, as well as suggest positive changes in your lifestyle choices.

What is included?

Measurements

These simple measures provide insight into your bodyweight category and how your body distributes fat to assess your risk of diseases such as cardiovascular, cancer and diabetes.

Blood tests

Carefully selected blood tests will give insight into how you metabolise glucose and fatty acids, and give an idea about your nutrient intake and nutritional status.

Nutrient analysis

Analysis of a 5-day food diary provides an accurate assessment of your nutritional intake. Inadequate or excessive nutrient intake can affect the risk of long-term health issues.

Lifestyle

Getting a picture of various aspects of your lifestyle including daily routine, sleep and physical activity helps to assess their influence on your health and well-being.

Consultation

During your consultation, we will discuss your medical history, any symptoms, medications and other relevant information to support a holistic view of your nutritional health.
