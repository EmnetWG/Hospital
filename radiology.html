Radiology & Imaging

Diagnostic Imaging Services covers a wide range of imaging or scanning tests, which produce images of the organs and structures inside the body that help in the investigation of patient symptoms or explore medical conditions. We provide imaging services to hospital inpatients, and also support outpatient investigations by referrals from other clinicians outside.

The services offered include:

  • Radiology/X-ray
  • Sonography (Ultrasound)
  • Mammography
  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • CT(Computerized Tomography) Scan
  • Echocardiography
  • Bone Density (DEXA/DXA - Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry)
  • Nuclear Medicine
  • PET/CT Scan
