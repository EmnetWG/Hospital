Orthopedics

The Orthopaedic unit is equipped with the state-of-the-art equipment and procedures for comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions of all ages. Our orthopedic surgeons use the latest approaches and minimally invasive techniques to provide the highest quality of care and the fastest return to active life. We offers advanced procedures in various fields.

The services offered include:

  • Spine and Back Care
  • Preservation Care
  • Foot and Ankle Surgery Services
  • Cartilage Restoration Services
  • Limb Preservation Services
  • Joint Replacement Services

Rehabilitation

  • Stroke & Neuro-Rehabilitation
  • Balance & Vertigo Rehabilitation
  • Facial Paralysis & Muscular Weakness
  • Cerebral Palsy Rehabilitation
  • Cardio-thoracic Rehabilitation
  • Sports Medicine, Sport Injuries and Arthroscopic Procedures of Shoulder, Knee, Elbow and Ankle Joints

When to Make an Appointment at the Orthopaedics Center

  • Chronic joint pain
  • Instability walking or standing
  • Pain in that starts in the low back and runs down one or both legs
  • Limited range of motion
  • Difficulty performing daily activities
