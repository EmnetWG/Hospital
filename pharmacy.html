Pharmacy

The pharmacy department can meet all medication needs, from prescriptions to over-the-counter medications for in-patient and out-patient. More than 6000 pharmaceutical products from various multinational companies available at the pharmacies. A wide variety of medical equipments, rehabilitation products, diagnostic products, lab apparatus from world renowned brands and manufacturers are also made available to the customer.

Clinical pharmacy teams, consisting of pharmacists and pharmacy technicians, provide advice to those prescribing medicines throughout the hospital to ensure that patients receive the best quality treatment for their care. This key role, known as medicines management, begins with coordinating the way in which medicines are selected, purchased and delivered to the hospital.
